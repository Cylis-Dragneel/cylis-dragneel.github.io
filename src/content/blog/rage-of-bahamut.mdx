# Review
<Callout type="info" title="Preface">
I do suggest you to read my notes as well after the review so you can witness my descend into insanity
</Callout>

This was a rollercoaster, a rollercoaster where the destination was in a ditch. Honestly, this had such great potential to be a fun anime, like Genesis was genuinely fun and then... virgin soul doesn't exist so I will start with genesis and the good.

## The good (genesis)
We had the gluttonous queen, Amira, an amazing brotherhood of love and hate between Kaiser and Favaro, the great funny duo of the Duck god Hamsa and the Drunk god Bacchus and Rita who is well Rita. The main highlight of the show was definitely in the OST and animation, with the studio being mappa, two of the openings being by SiM and overall OST was just absolutely peak chefskiss. While the story wasn't the most outstanding, I did have a few wtf moments namely
<Spoiler title="Martinet">
me going from thinking Lucifer is Amira's father, then seeing Martinet in the flashback thinking "Yea that looks like Lucifer", to then learning about Lazalley having the amulet thinking he is the father, then learning that Lazalley was just a bodyguard so going back to the theory that Lucifer is the father, to then seeing Martinet for the first time and knowing he isn't Lucifer so ok he has to be the father right? Then this man pulls the "All your memories were fabricated by me" card and to top it all off "Beelzebub was more like your mother than your birth mother" card just fucking insane
</Spoiler>
Genuinely, with all of the aspects I liked about genensis it was a great anime with the exception of a somehwat lacking story and at times 1 dimensional characters... Like I still want to see Bacchus' entire backstory with how he got kicked out etc. And I do have to note here too I really loved the short stories as well, we got proper closure to Kaiser and Favaro then we got some bond building with Jeanne and Kaiser it was nice and wholesome.

## The negatives (virgin soul)
All of Virgin Soul.
I think off the top of my head the only positive thing I can remember about Virgin Soul that isn't the music or the fights.... Is Azazel, genuinely he became my favorite character in Virgin Soul... everyone else just... existed. Nina is the worst character I have had to follow as a main character, there's nothing good about her character I like. The king is just a piece of shit villain with no redeeming qualities, why? Because it doesn't matter if he played football with those demon kids when he caused their entire species to be persecuted and enslaved. Nothing he could do can make up for anything and the fact that Nina still continues to.... yea just things that gave me headaches. I daresay I haven't seen such a big fumble since Promise Neverland Season 2, cause this is the biggest disappointment I have had since then. And to be clear, my outlook on Virgin soul wouldn't change for the better if I hadn't watched Genesis, in fact I would rate Virgin Soul even worse as a standalone. It is only this high because of Genesis' legacy and the characters. Thus, I would like to pretend Virgin Soul never even existed because hell... the ending of the anime sure disregards 90% of what happened because Bahamut didn't die, the genocide never had a point to begin with, and we still have a tyrant on the throne

Overall, I'd give Genesis a 8/10, the Short Stories a 7.5/10 and Virgin Soul a 4/10.
For a final score of: 5/10
